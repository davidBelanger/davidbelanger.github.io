David Belanger's Research Homepage













I am a research scientist on the Google Brain Team. I recently graduated from UMass Amherst, where I was advised by Professor Andrew McCallum. My grad school research spanned graphical models, structured prediction, and deep learning. I have interned with Sham Kakade at Microsoft Research and Dilip Krishnan at Google. I also co-organized the weekly UMass Machine Learning and Friends Lunch for 5 years. I am interested in a wide range of machine learning methods and applications. Reach out to me if you want to chat!
